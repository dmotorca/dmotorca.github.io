HELLO ALL

I am David Motorca and I prefer the pronouns he/him. I have been at PSU since 2019 and will hopefully be graduating this year. My ultimate job direction would be in an industry that is beneficial to the climate or environment. I understand that this direction is incredibly vague which is true but simply due to the fact that I don't really have a good idea for what exact industry/company I want to work at. Over this last summer, I interned at TigerStop in Washington, it was a great experience that really broadened my horizon/passion for embedded systems work. The company specializes in creating machines that mitigate waste material while simultaneously increasing production. I got to work on both the front-end and back-end for the project which was really awesome as I got to fully incorporate changes with the exact vision that I had. In addition, the application was launched on a linux system which was another challenge but is now ultimately a strength of mine as I am very comfortable developing on linux. I am looking forward to go skiing on Mount Hood this upcoming winter. During the summer months I really enjoy going on overnight backpacking trips. I also really enjoy playing video games whenever I have free time. I am currently playing Overwatch2 and I am looking forward to the release of God of War Ragnarok.
