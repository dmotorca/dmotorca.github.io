Previous Work

TigerStop is a company that specializes in creating machines and devices to simplfy work and reduce the amount of waste from materials. At the internship I specifically worked on creating their next SawGear product which included working on both the frontend and the backend while deploying the device on a linux based system. One of my most proud achievements during this internship, was the fact that during alpha development, I had found a solution to automatically download excel files that contained a parts-list for a certain job. This allowed a user to simply insert a USB stick and press a button to import a parts-list and the machine would simply start work based on these instructions. The version of the alpha that had this contribution was then displayed at the largest woodworking show in North America (IWF Atlanta).
